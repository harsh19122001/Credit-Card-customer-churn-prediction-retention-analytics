# 🏦 Credit Card Customer Churn Prediction & Retention Analytics
Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges for banks and financial institutions. Losing existing customers directly impacts revenue and increases customer acquisition costs.

This project focuses on identifying customers who are likely to leave the bank and providing data-driven retention strategies before churn occurs.

🎯 Business Problem

The bank had limited visibility into:

Which customers are likely to churn
Key factors driving customer attrition
Customer segments requiring retention efforts
How to reduce customer loss and improve retention

The objective was to build a predictive analytics solution that helps identify high-risk customers and support proactive retention strategies.

🔍 Key Business Insights
Customer Activity Matters

Customers with higher inactivity periods showed greater churn risk.

Transaction Behavior

Lower transaction count and transaction amount were strongly associated with customer attrition.

Credit Utilization

Credit usage patterns helped differentiate retained customers from churned customers.

Customer Engagement

Highly engaged customers were significantly more likely to remain with the bank.
